# Assignment 2: Footballers App Continued...

This is an app named the **Footballers App** as it allows you to create different football teams and inside them
create your own players, go crazy with custom teams or recreate some of your all time favourites.
The Teams tab contains your created teams and inside contains the players you create. Click on the About tab to find
out more and view your own personal staistics. The search tab allows you to sort through your teams by League.

The app can be viewed on any device as it scales automatically, personally I think a mobile view looks the nicest.
